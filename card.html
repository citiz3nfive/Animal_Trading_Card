Silverback Gorilla

Silverback gorillas can weigh more than 200kg and measure up to six feet tall. Their large jaw muscles are utilised for chewing tough vegetation.

  • Scientific Name: Gorilla
  • Average Size: 5 feet to 5.9 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 35 years
  • Habitat: Tropical Rainforest

An average silverback gorilla can weigh up to 180kg (that’s almost 30 stone), and measure 170cm (over 5’5”) tall on all fours. In contrast, female mountain gorillas weigh 90kg, and measure up to 150cm (4’9”).
